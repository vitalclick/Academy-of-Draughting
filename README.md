# The Academy of Advanced Draughting — AI-Powered Website Redesign

## Overview

The Academy of Advanced Draughting website is being transformed from a traditional informational education website into a modern AI-powered digital ecosystem focused on:

* Intelligent student recruitment
* Automated admissions
* AI-driven engagement
* Career-focused education experiences
* Smart personalization
* Operational automation
* Future-ready engineering education delivery

This platform is designed to position The Academy of Advanced Draughting as a leading innovation-driven engineering and draughting institution in South Africa and beyond.

---

# Vision

The future of educational websites is no longer static information pages.

This platform is designed to function as:

> An intelligent engineering career development ecosystem powered by AI and industry-focused training.

The website must:

* Engage visitors intelligently
* Guide students through enrollment
* Automate repetitive processes
* Improve operational efficiency
* Enhance student decision-making
* Increase conversion rates
* Support scalable digital education

---

# Institution Background

## The Academy of Advanced Draughting

Established in 1981, The Academy of Advanced Draughting specializes in:

* Draughting education
* CAD software training
* Engineering drawing
* Design office workflows
* Industry-focused technical training

The institution focuses on preparing students for real engineering and design office environments through practical and career-aligned learning.
