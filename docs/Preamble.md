## Project: The Academy of Advanced Draughting — AI-Powered Website Transformation

You are responsible for redesigning and redeveloping the official website of **The Academy of Advanced Draughting**, a South African specialist draughting education institution established in 1981.

This is NOT a basic educational website redesign.

The objective is to transform the platform into a:

* Modern AI-powered digital ecosystem
* Intelligent student recruitment platform
* Career-focused engineering education hub
* Automated admissions and engagement system
* Future-ready educational technology platform

The final product must compete visually and functionally with world-class modern SaaS, AI, and EdTech platforms.

---

# PRIMARY OBJECTIVES

The new website must:

1. Increase student enrollment conversions
2. Improve trust and authority
3. Automate admissions workflows
4. Deliver AI-powered user experiences
5. Improve SEO and AI discoverability
6. Enhance engagement and retention
7. Modernize the institution’s digital identity
8. Support future expansion into online learning
9. Reduce manual administrative workload
10. Position the institution as an innovation leader in engineering education
